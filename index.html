🏔️ Area Guide — Northern Colorado I-25 Corridor

A quick orientation to the communities in your search area, to help narrow where you'd most like to focus.

🌟 Firestone & Frederick

These two adjacent small cities along CO-52 offer the sweet spot of new construction, reasonable prices, and fast I-25 access. Both are incorporated municipalities with good schools tied to St. Vrain Valley School District.

  • 20–25 min to Boulder, 30 min to Fort Collins via I-25
  • New development areas offer modern floor plans & warranties
  • Lower property taxes than Boulder County communities
  • Quiet, suburban feel — growing but not overdeveloped
  • Best for: New construction, value, easy highway access

🏙️ Longmont

A full-service city of ~100,000 with a vibrant downtown, diverse dining scene, Lyons-style character, and significantly larger lot sizes. Longmont offers older character homes alongside newer developments.

  • Established neighborhoods with mature trees and larger lots
  • Strong arts & culture scene, independent restaurants
  • St. Vrain Valley Schools — consistently top-rated
  • Good access to Boulder (20 min via CO-119)
  • Best for: Character, community feel, lot size

🏘️ Dacono

A small, affordable community between Firestone and Longmont. Primarily newer residential development with good I-25 access. Home prices run slightly lower than Frederick/Firestone for comparable homes.

  • Lower price-per-sq-ft than neighboring Firestone
  • Quiet, residential — less commercial development nearby
  • Easy commute access north and south on I-25
  • Best for: Maximum space for budget

🌿 Erie

Erie sits at the crossroads of Boulder, Broomfield, and Weld counties — one of Colorado's fastest-growing cities. Premium feel with excellent trails, newer schools, and a growing town center.

  • Top-rated Boulder Valley & St. Vrain school access
  • Coal Creek Trail and open space connectivity
  • Erie Town Center with restaurants, events, market
  • Slightly higher HOA culture — most communities gated/governed
  • Best for: Premium suburban experience, schools, trails
